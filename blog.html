How we choose our daily challenges

Each day’s puzzle is selected using a simple algorithm based on the current date. This ensures that everyone sees the same challenge and can compare results with friends. In a future version we plan to integrate AI so puzzles adapt to your skill level. AI‑powered recommendations have been shown to significantly increase user engagement, which means better experiences for you and more sustainable revenues for us.
