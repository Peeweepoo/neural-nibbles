About

Byte‑Sized Brains is a passion project designed to give you a quick mental workout every day. We believe that keeping your mind active shouldn’t require a huge time investment. Our micro‑challenges take only a few minutes to complete, but they spark curiosity and build mental agility.

This site is also an experiment in creating sustainable digital businesses. High‑quality content sites attract loyal readers who return regularly, and forums or support groups build vibrant communities. We use advertising to support the free version of the site; ad networks suggest that sites in popular niches can earn anywhere from \$1,000 to over \$50,000 per month depending on traffic and optimization. Over time, we plan to offer a premium tier without ads and with additional analytics and challenge archives.

If you’d like to extend this prototype, head over to the project repository and modify the puzzle list in script.js. You can deploy the static files on services like Netlify or GitHub Pages, or integrate a back‑end to support user accounts, AI‑generated puzzles and real advertising. We hope this inspires you to create your own micro‑content business!
